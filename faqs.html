Frequently Asked Questions

Will I need a car?

It's up to you, but we planned everything so a car wouldn't be necessary. The hotel is close to DCA airport (5 miles/8 minute car ride), the nearest metro stop (0.2 miles, 5 minute walk), and the venue (1.1 miles, 23 minute walk, 4 minute car ride).

The venue is a neighborhood park, and there is street parking. However, self parking at the hotel is $26 per day (as of August 2023). For the most recent list of hotel amenities check the hotel website directly.

What is the dress code?

Cocktail attire, sometimes called semi-formal attire. The event will be partially outdoors (on grass!), so feel free to wear something comfortable enough to walk and dance in.

Do you have a registry?

Yes, we have a registry at Zola and Amazon, with more information below. However, sharing our day with you — in person or in spirit — is more than enough of a gift!

OK, where is your registry information?

At Zola we have some cash funds setup. You can send us cash with no transaction fee for the Venmo-driven funds, or with a small fee (we will cover this) on the credit card-driven funds. Of course there's traditional physical checks and cash which incur no fees.

At Amazon we have set up a registry with an eclectic listing of gifts we would love. If you're so inclined, you could also buy us these gifts from a small online retailer and avoid buying via Amazon; if you do so just kindly mark your gift as purchased on the registry, so we don't receive duplicates (e.g. 4 AeroPress coffee presses).
